About Me

Lumenpulse Embedded Systems Co-op

At Lumenpulse I worked on several microcontroller projects as part of the firmware team. I also spent time workng with Altium for schematic design and board layout. My main projects were Lumenfacade Rev 6, CBOX and Lumentone.

I designed the PCB layout for the Lumenfacade board through several iterations. This included several hardware changes in the schematics, and significant changes to the board layout.

The CBOX directs communication over DMX and RDM. I worked both of these protocols to fix exsiting issues with interference coming into the receiver.

The picture to the left is from a R&D project to incorportate an OLED into a lumentone light controller. Usign the STM32F030R8 development board I prototyped a set up to control lights over DMX with an interactive menu displayed on the OLED screen.

Draper Electrical Engineering Co-op

At Draper I worked with the Sensors and Imaging Systems group. I was responsible for the electrical system in a pod test set up. I also worked with two other co-ops on Wireless Sensor Network Research.

I spent a lot of time working with OrCAD capture to review PCB schematics and order parts for the manufacturer. I also used OrCAD to compile wiring diagrams for the pod project.

Researching wireless sensor networks with the other co-ops led our team to develop a project focused on camera based networks. After spending a significant amount of time reading existing journal publications we chose to focus on surveillance using a network of cameras.
